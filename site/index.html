About Our Project

Our project’s goal is to figure out how the people of the United States felt during different points in the pandemic (lockdowns and other rules) and how certain decisions that were made by government officials affected those emotions. In studying these emotions we hope that we can help outline the effects of certain decisions made, in order to be better prepared if something like this would occur again in the future. We are working on a project where we do a sentiment analysis on different social media sites (Twitter, Facebook, and Reddit) in order to discern responses that the United States felt during different periods of the pandemic. The goal in using 3 different applications is to see if there are any discernible difference of sentiment between them. In the end, we will build a website in order to host all of our findings.

For ISTA 498 - Capstone by Rincon, Thomas O’Connell, Harris, Zvijer, Barraza
